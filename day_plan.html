Plan My Day

Streetart

Street Art Platanenweg

You will see some amazing streetart on the sides of multiple buidlings. These artworks are part of a project called "If walls could speak" in 2019. There are in total 10 artworks to be seen on 5 buildings. Can you find them all?

Don't forget to scan/open the spotify QR-code to listen to a playlist that fits your day plan trip. Enjoy!

Shopping

Oostpoort Mall

During the day, you will also visit the Oostpoort Mall. You can compare this like a small village, but then with shops. These kind of malls are very common in The Netherlands. You can find here shops where you can shop for fashion, lifestyle and groceries.

Cafés

Caffe Milo

Caffe Milo has a great ssortiment of food and drinks. You can sit on their terrace and in their cozy café-restaurant. Here you can try their all-day kitchen, cocktails, fine wines and a wide range of beer, including several Italian beers.
